HOW TO BAKE A CAKE

Ingredients needed

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Butter
  • Baking powder
  • Eggs

Steps on how to bake a cake

  1. Mix the dry ingredients i.e Flour and Baking soda
  2. Mix the butter and sugar in one direction until it forms a smooth paste
  3. Proceed to mix the egg with the butter-sugar mixture
  4. Now mix the wet and dry ingredients then add the vanilla extract
  5. You can now preheat your oven at about 1500c
  6. Dust your baking pan with butter and flour then pour in your mixture
  7. Put the mixture in the oven and let bake for a while before doing the toothpick test
